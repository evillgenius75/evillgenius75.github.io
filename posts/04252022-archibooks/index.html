Suggested Readings, Links, Podcasts and other Sundry Items for the Burgeoning Cloud Architect

April 25, 2022

Here I present a list of resources I have used or am curretnly using to further my knowldege in the Application Architecture space with a purposeful focus on Cloud Native developement and Distributed Systems design. I would love to hear of other resources, you my reader, may have found useful in your journey in Modern Application design.
